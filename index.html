The Problem

Too Slow

Current optical modulators operate at >100ms, limiting network efficiency and data center performance.

Inefficient

Optical efficiency below 40% results in signal loss, crosstalk, and higher power consumption.

Technical Limitations

Fringing field effects, poor channel isolation, and bandwidth constraints limit performance.
